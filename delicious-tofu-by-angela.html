Summary:

Delicious Tofu: Recipes and Lore from the Land of Tofu is a well-researched and carefully-crafted cookbook, written by a native tofu chef. She was born in China and raised in Taiwan, where tofu is inseparable from the culinary landscape. The following features are what make this book stand out.

  • It offers an unprecedented mixture of authentic information with engaging stories from Chinese history, legends, and current tofu culture for reading pleasure, as well as helpful culinary background.
  • It presents a wide variety of tasty dishes which illustrate that tofu can appeal to meat lovers as well as vegetarians.
  • It introduced traditional and creative recipes using a wide range of cooking methods. Readers will find a rich repertoire of little known techniques, tips, shortcuts, and even global tofu dining guides.
  • It reveals a variety of prepared tofu products currently found in today’s food market – a valuable source even native tofu cooks overlook. These tofu ‘jewels’ come pre-seasoned and ready to serve and are delicious, healthy and convenient options worthy of exploration.
